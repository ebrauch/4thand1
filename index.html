About

As fantasy football fanatics, we are constantly looking for tools to give us an edge on our competition. We always seem to find apps that are riddled with subjectivity and provide little or no actual player statistics.

Exhibit A:

For those who don't want to expend much mental energy in determining their lineups, this is great. There's even a green box around the player with higher projections than the other. For those of us who enjoy the process of gathering statistics and information to make decisions, this is borderline completely useless.

This is where 4thand1 comes into play. Player statistics are broken down into location-specific categories, and displayed in a manner which makes it easy to interpret. Simply hover over an area of the field, and you will be presented with pertinent statistics to ensure you make the right roster decisions.
